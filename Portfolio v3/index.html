Awards and Honors

DECA 2023 SCDC ASM Top 10 Finalist

DECA 2023 SCDC ASM Top 10 Finalist

Each year, all states organize a State Career Development Conference (SCDC) to serve as a qualifying event for the prestigious International Career Developmental Conference (ICDC).

In 2023, during her freshman year, Shuhan Li took part in the Automotive Services Marketing Series event. Demonstrating her skills and dedication, she achieved the commendable position of a Top 10 Finalist at the SCDC.

Katy Taylor 2022 Public Forum Champion

Katy Taylor 2022 Public Forum Champion

In 2022, during Shuhan Li's freshman year, she participated in the Public Forum debate at the Katy Taylor tournament in the novice division.

Alongside her partner Pranjal Kaushal, they achieved a 9-2 record throughout the tournament. In the finals, they secured a victory with a 3-0 vote.

Ida B Wells 2022 Public Forum Co-Champion

Ida B Wells 2022 Public Forum Co-Champion

In her second tournament of the season, Shuhan Li teamed up with Rishab Jain to compete in the Public Forum event at Ida B Wells High School.

Their skills and efforts led them to reach the semi-finals, an impressive achievement. However, a situation arose where all the other semi-finalists were from the same school. As a result, a decision was made to designate all the semi-finalists as co-champions.

Throughout the tournament, Shuhan and Rishab displayed a strong performance, securing a perfect record of 3-0.
